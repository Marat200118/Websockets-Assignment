Remote snake - Made with Websockets 🐍

Open this page on your mobile device and use it as a controller to play the game on your desktop.

Instructions 📑

1. Scan the QR code with your phone

2. Press start button, to start the game

3. Controll the snake with buttons or gyroscope

4. Have fun and try to beat me!
P.S My record is 590

Some tips and tricks 👻

1. For better gyroscope experience disable the auto-rotate features

2. Hold your phone parallel to the ground

3. After rotating on one side, return phone to start position
